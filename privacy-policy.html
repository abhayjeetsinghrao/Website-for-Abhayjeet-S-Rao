Privacy Policy

Office of Abhayjeet Singh Rao — Counsel  ·  Effective Date: 6 June 2026  ·  New Delhi, India

This Privacy Policy explains how the Office of Abhayjeet Singh Rao, Counsel ("we", "our", or "the Office") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information submitted through this website (officeofabhayjeetsinghrao.com). We are committed to handling your information with the discretion and confidentiality that every professional legal engagement demands.

Section 01

Information We Collect

When you use the contact form or the DraftDesk document-request service on this website, you may provide the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity data: Your full name.
  • Contact data: Email address and phone number.
  • Matter data: A brief description of your legal matter or document requirement, including any facts or context you choose to share.
  • Service data: Type of document requested and urgency preference (DraftDesk submissions).

We do not collect any data automatically through cookies or tracking technologies beyond the standard analytics and advertising tags described in Section 5 below.

Section 02

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry or consultation request.
  • To prepare, review, and deliver legal drafts or documents requested through DraftDesk.
  • To communicate quotes, timelines, and follow-up correspondence relevant to your matter.
  • To comply with professional obligations under the Advocates Act, 1961 and Bar Council of India Rules.

We do not use your personal information for marketing, promotional communications, or any purpose unrelated to your specific enquiry, unless you separately consent.

Section 03

Confidentiality & Professional Privilege

All communications with this Office are treated as strictly confidential. Information you share regarding a legal matter is protected by professional obligation and, where applicable, by legal professional privilege under Indian law. We do not disclose, sell, rent, or share your information with any third party except:

  • Where required by law, court order, or competent authority.
  • Where necessary to engage a briefing counsel or co-counsel in your specific matter, and only with your knowledge.
  • Where you have expressly consented to the disclosure.
Section 04

Form Submissions & Third-Party Processing

Contact and DraftDesk form submissions on this website are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and then forwarded to us by email. Formspree's own privacy policy governs their handling of that data.

We encourage you to review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy before submitting sensitive information. For highly sensitive matters, we recommend contacting us directly by phone or email.

Section 05

Cookies & Analytics

This website uses the following tracking technologies:

  • Google Ads (AW-18195003224): A conversion tracking tag that records when a visitor completes an action such as submitting a contact form. This helps us measure the effectiveness of our advertising.
  • Google Site Verification: A non-tracking verification token used to confirm website ownership with Google Search Console.

No other cookies, session storage, or tracking scripts are currently deployed. You may disable tracking through your browser settings or a browser extension such as uBlock Origin without affecting any website functionality.

Section 06

Data Retention

We retain personal information submitted through this website for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Respond to and conclude your enquiry or matter.
  • Comply with applicable legal, tax, and professional record-keeping obligations.
  • Resolve any dispute that may arise in connection with your matter.

Enquiries that do not proceed to an engagement are typically deleted within 12 months of the last communication. Matter-related records may be retained for up to 7 years in accordance with standard professional practice.

Section 07

Your Rights

Under applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (once in force), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request erasure of data where retention is no longer necessary, subject to legal hold obligations.
  • Withdraw consent to processing where processing is based solely on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 9 below.

Section 08

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to protect personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. The website is served over HTTPS (SSL/TLS). However, no method of electronic transmission is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. For matters involving sensitive facts, we recommend direct communication rather than web forms.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The current version will always be available at officeofabhayjeetsinghrao.com/privacy-policy.html with the effective date noted at the top. Continued use of this website following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
